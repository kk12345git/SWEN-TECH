1. The Myth of the "Cheap" Template

Generic ERP templates are designed to fit 80% of businesses. For a specialized manufacturer, that remaining 20% gap is where your profit margin lives. When your software doesn't perfectly mirror your physical production floor, your staff ends up working for the software (manual workarounds, Excel exports) instead of the software working for you.

2. Technical Debt vs. Engineering Wealth

Custom solutions like our Production OS are built on modular, high-load architectures (Python, Django, PostgreSQL) that scale as you add machine lines. Templates, conversely, trap you in "feature silos" where adding a custom OEE tracking module might be impossible or prohibitively expensive via legacy plugins.
