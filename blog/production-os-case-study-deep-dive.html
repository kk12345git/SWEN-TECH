How We Built a Production OS That Increased Efficiency by 30%

January 20, 2026 12 min read

In the competitive landscape of medical manufacturing, data is as critical as the hardware itself. For KRG Medifabb, the challenge wasn't just production—it was visibility.

The Architecture of Efficiency

When we first audited the production workflow at KRG Medifabb, we found that nearly 40% of management's time was spent on manual synchronization. Spreadsheets were the primary "database," leading to inevitable drift between what was on paper and what was on the factory floor.

Solving with Modern Tech

We chose a stack composed of **FastAPI** for its high-performance asynchronous execution and **React** for a responsive, real-time dashboard. By implementing a centralized PostgreSQL database with automated costing algorithms, we eliminated the need for manual calculations.

"Digital transformation isn't about replacing processes; it's about amplifying them with data-driven intelligence."

Key Results

  • 30% Reduction in order processing time.
  • 95% Accuracy in inventory forecasting.
  • Real-time Visibility across 10+ factory stations.
