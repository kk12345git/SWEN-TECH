SEO Best Practices for B2B Websites in 2026

January 24, 2026 7 min read

SEO in 2026 is no longer about keyword stuffing; it's about semantic authority and technical performance.

Core Web Vitals are Non-Negotiable

Google's algorithms now prioritize sites that provide a seamless user experience. If your site has a high Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) time, you're invisible regardless of your content quality.

Structural Data and Schema

For B2B players like Srivari Alloys, implementing Product Schema and Organization Schema allows Google to understand your catalog as structured data, often leading to rich snippets that increase CTR by 25%.

Content Depth vs. GPT-Sludge

With the flood of AI-generated content, search engines are rewarding "Information Gain"—content that provides new facts or uniquely deep insights from real-world experience.
