01. The Challenge

Srivari Alloys had been a trusted name in Chennai's alloy manufacturing sector for years. But their digital presence told a completely different story — an outdated, slow-loading website that looked like it was built in 2010, with no mobile responsiveness, no product catalogue, and no clear way for B2B buyers to enquire.

With competitors improving their online presence and buyers increasingly researching suppliers online before calling, the Managing Director knew it was time for a complete rebuild.

02. What We Built

🏢
Premium Homepage
Bold hero section, company highlights, trust signals, and a clear enquiry CTA above the fold.
📦
Product Catalogue
Organised product category pages with technical specifications, photos, and WhatsApp enquiry links per product.
📈
SEO Foundation
Proper meta tags, structured data, page speed optimisation, and Google Search Console setup from day one.
📱
Mobile-First Design
60% of their buyers browse on mobile. We designed mobile-first, then scaled up to desktop.
